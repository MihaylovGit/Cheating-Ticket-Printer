Cheating The Ticket Printer

Bus drivers found a "creative" way to cheat the fiscal printers by opening their paper lid before the ticket is completely printed, thereby forcing the printer to start printing the ticket again. The printer was assuming it was out of paper so it started printing the ticket from the beginning. This way drivers sold the same ticket multiple times to multiple customer stealing the money for these tickets.

The company discovered the trick a couple of months later after inspecting a video feed. Fortunately each line printed was recorded on the memory of the printer so we are able to analyze it and discover how much money each driver stole.

Your task is to analyze the printer output and provide the number of duplicate sales and net impact. Note that first sale is legitimate, every next sale counts as duplicate. Each ticket may have a different price depending on the line and bus stop.
